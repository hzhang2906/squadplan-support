Frequently Asked Questions

How do I share a calendar?

Open the calendar, tap the Share button, and send the invite link to anyone you want to join.

Is my data private?

Yes. SquadPlan uses Apple iCloud to sync your data. It is stored on your device and your personal iCloud account — never on third-party servers.

How do I delete my data?

Go to Settings → Delete All Data inside the app. This permanently removes all your data from the app and iCloud.

Which devices are supported?

SquadPlan requires iOS 17 or later.
